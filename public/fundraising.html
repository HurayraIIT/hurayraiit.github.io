About the Masjid Project

Our village is called Chamragacha, located in Gobindganj Upazila, Gaibandha District. The old masjid building has become unsafe and cannot accommodate our growing community.

Our grandfather has donated a large plot of land for the new masjid construction. May Allah accept his donation. We filled the pond on the new land and prepared the foundation after demolishing the old structure.

Currently, we are praying in a temporary thatched masjid. Most people in our village live below the poverty line and cannot bear the construction costs alone.

Quranic Reference: "And [mention, O Muhammad], when We made the House a place of return for the people and [a place of] security. And take, [O believers], from the standing place of Abraham a place of prayer." (Surah Al-Baqarah: 125)
